📌 What is Revenue Leakage?

Revenue leakage in the City of Harare occurs when billable income fails to reach the municipal treasury due to manual collection gaps, unreconciled payments, fraudulent waivers, duplicate billing, and ghost ratepayers. This system detects leakage through anomaly detection algorithms, reconciliation tracking, and real-time alert monitoring — enabling officers to act before losses become permanent.
